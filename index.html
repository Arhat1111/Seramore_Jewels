About the CEO

Led with a vision for graceful modern jewellery.

The CEO of Seramore Jewels leads the brand with a simple belief: jewellery should feel personal, premium and timeless. The focus is on building a brand that values trust, refined taste and customer relationships over mass online selling.

Every piece is presented with attention to styling, detail and emotion, helping customers discover jewellery that feels meaningful for gifting, occasions and everyday elegance.

About the Company

Seramore Jewels, crafted in Surat for a refined jewellery experience.

Seramore Jewels is a new jewellery startup based in Surat, Gujarat. The brand is designed for customers who appreciate soft luxury, clean presentation and personal consultation before making a jewellery decision.

Instead of operating like a regular ecommerce store, Seramore uses its website as a visual portfolio. Visitors can explore the brand mood, view jewellery work, browse the gallery and contact the team directly for bookings, inquiries or private previews.
