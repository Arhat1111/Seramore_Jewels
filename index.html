The Vision
Behind the Craft

Mr. Rajesh Mehta brings over two decades of expertise in the diamond and jewellery trade. A Surat native and second-generation jeweller, he has shaped Seramore into a brand that honours tradition while embracing contemporary elegance.

Under his stewardship, Seramore has grown from a local atelier to a name trusted across India and beyond — built on integrity, craftsmanship, and an uncompromising eye for detail.

How We Work

The Seramore
Craft Process

Every Seramore piece journeys through five stages of meticulous creation — from the first spark of a design idea to the moment it rests in your hands.

Step 01
01

Design & Concept

Concept sketches, CAD modelling, and client consultations shape the vision. We translate your ideas into precise technical drawings.

Step 02
02

Stone Sourcing

Ethically sourced, certified diamonds and gemstones hand-selected for cut, clarity, colour, and carat to meet our exacting standards.

Step 03
03

Wax & Casting

Precision wax modelling followed by lost-wax casting in 18kt or 22kt gold, performed by master craftsmen in our Surat studio.

Step 04
04

Stone Setting

Expert stone setting — prong, pavé, channel, and bezel — executed under 10× magnification by our specialist setters.

Step 05
05

Finishing & QC

Polishing, rhodium plating, quality inspection, hallmarking, and secure luxury packaging — ensuring every piece meets the Seramore standard.
