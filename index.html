Our Story

Where Surat's Heritage
Meets Timeless Design

Born in the diamond capital of the world, Seramore was founded with a singular vision — to transform Surat's world-class gemstone craftsmanship into jewellery that transcends occasion and time.

Every piece that leaves our atelier carries the soul of our city — disciplined, brilliant, and crafted by hands that have mastered their art over generations.

Leadership

The Vision
Behind the Craft

Mr. Rajesh Mehta brings over two decades of expertise in the diamond and jewellery trade. A Surat native and second-generation jeweller, he has shaped Seramore into a brand that honours tradition while embracing contemporary elegance.

Under his stewardship, Seramore has grown from a local atelier to a name trusted across India and beyond — built on integrity, craftsmanship, and an uncompromising eye for detail.

"Jewellery is not merely adornment. It is memory made tangible — a gift that carries the weight of love, celebration, and legacy. At Seramore, we craft not just pieces, but heirlooms."

Rajesh Mehta

How We Work

The Seramore
Craft Process

Every Seramore piece journeys through five stages of meticulous creation — from the first spark of a design idea to the moment it rests in your hands.

01

The Craft Process

01

Design &
Concept

Concept sketches, CAD modelling, and client consultations shape the vision. We translate your ideas into precise technical drawings before a single gram of gold is touched.

02

The Craft Process

02

Stone
Sourcing

Ethically sourced, certified diamonds and gemstones are hand-selected for cut, clarity, colour, and carat. Every stone we use meets GIA and IGI certification standards.

03

The Craft Process

03

Wax &
Casting

Precision wax modelling followed by lost-wax casting in 18kt or 22kt gold, performed by master craftsmen in our Surat studio using time-honoured techniques.

04

The Craft Process

04

Stone
Setting

Expert stone setting — prong, pavé, channel, and bezel — executed under 10× magnification by our specialist setters with decades of precision experience.

05

The Craft Process

05

Finishing
& Quality

Polishing, rhodium plating, quality inspection, hallmarking, and secure luxury packaging — every piece signed off before it leaves our atelier.
